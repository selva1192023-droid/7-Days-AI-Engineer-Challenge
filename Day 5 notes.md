# Day 5 - Generative AI & LLMs

## What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that creates new content such as text, images, audio, videos, and code.

## What are LLMs?

LLMs (Large Language Models) are AI models trained on massive amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like responses.

Examples:

* ChatGPT
* Gemini
* Claude
* Llama
