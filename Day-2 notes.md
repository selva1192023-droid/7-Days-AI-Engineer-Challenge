## Why Python for AI?

Python is the most popular programming language for Artificial Intelligence because of its simplicity and powerful libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.

## Key Takeaway

Python forms the foundation of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Science, and Generative AI.
